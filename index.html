Rules

  • Respect Admins and their decisions.
  • Cheating is not allowed. Including abuse of battle flags.
  • Only English language in Global chat & General chat. Keep it decent.
  • No Building to block important spawns and single entrance.
  • No fence stacking and ceiling stacking.
  • No foundation/Item spam. Landclaim must be done by practical builds.
  • Max 5 maprooms per solo player or clan.
  • Avoid unnecessary large claim.
  • Players are advised to construct aesthetically pleasing buildings/structures as its a PvE server.
  • Avoid unnecessary wheel of pain bases.
  • Boss traps are illegal.
  • Reporting rule breaks must be done through a ticket and be supported by video evidence, not to admins directly.
  • Thrall wheels must be removed when not in use.

Quests

Our lovely Conan is missing quests, a lot of them. And we all know they are of great importance to the game storry and gameplay. On our server Exodia Gaming, we have two people who are actively making quests, I(Exodia) and Ashira who is an Admin.

We have ideas but as you can probably guess the quest writing river in our head sometimes is getting dry. Sometimes ideas for quests seem so boring and repetitive. So I'm asking You the player or anyone who visits our website if you have a story for a quest to Join our Discord server and share your story for a quest.

Of course you will get some perks, for example, you will name your guy/gal/something between quest giver, discord ranks and more.
